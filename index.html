Topptur GPX Overlay

Overlay your GPX tracks on topptur.guide

iPhone / iPad (Bookmarklet)

No app or extension needed. Works in Safari.

1 Bookmark this page
Tap the share button and choose "Add Bookmark". Save it anywhere.
2 Edit the bookmark
Open your bookmarks, find the one you just saved, and tap Edit. Replace the URL with this code:

GPX Overlay

On desktop: drag the button above to your bookmarks bar.
On iPhone: copy the code below and paste it as the bookmark URL.


javascript:void(fetch('https://janthemanheier.github.io/randonee-overlay/gpx-overlay.user.js').then(r=>r.text()).then(t=>eval(t.replace(/^\/\/.*==UserScript==[\s\S]*?\/\/.*==\/UserScript==/,''))))
3 Use it
Go to topptur.guide, wait for the map to load, then open your bookmarks and tap GPX Overlay. Your tracks will appear on the map.

Desktop (Tampermonkey)

For automatic loading every time you visit topptur.guide:

1 Install Tampermonkey browser extension
2 Click here to install the userscript

The script auto-updates when new versions are pushed to GitHub.
